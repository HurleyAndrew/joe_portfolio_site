Joseph Hurley is an animator hailing from Connecticut. He has always admired cartoons ever since his days as a little baby boy, and taking his first steps in animating his own work during highschool. Throughout his animated projects, Joe continues to look for unique ways to express humor particularly though pushing the comedic expressions and designs of animated characters and by fine tuning the timing on how each shot plays out. He has made several animated works in his spare time from original to parody. Joe is currently a grad student at Rochester Institute of Technology studying Film and Animation and graduated from Roger Williams University in Visual Arts. His first film while at RIT was Cellular Distraction He is also proficient in illustration, voice acting, graphic design, and is a passionate cross country runner.
